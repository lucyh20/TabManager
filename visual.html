How To Use the Visualizer

To use the visualizer, click the ICON and enter a search query. As you browse webpages, highlight any information you want to remember, then press Alt+1, Alt+2, Alt+3 to save that information as high priority, medium priority or low priority, respectively. Anytime you open a new tab, this page will appear with the option to view your information in a list or a map view.
