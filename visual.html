Tab Manager

Need help?

To use Tab Mangager, click the circular and enter a search query.
As you open and view different tabs in your web browser, press:
    Alt+1 to save an important website,
    Alt+2 to mark a website as potentially important to which you want to return to later,
    Alt+3 to note a website you viewed that turned out to be unimportant.
The links to these websites will be saved below for you to return to at anytime in the future.
Anytime you open a new tab, this page will appear with the websites you have added. You may also click the circular to see your list while in a tab for a different website.
