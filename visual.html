How To Use the Visualizer

To use the visualizer, click the ICON and enter a search query.

As you browse webpages, highlight any information you want to remember, then press Alt+1, Alt+2, Alt+3 to save potentially important information, to archive important information, and to note information that had potential but turned out to be irrelevant.

Anytime you open a new tab, this page will appear with the option to view your information in a list view.
